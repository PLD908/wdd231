Individual Project Site Plan

Site Name

Mountain Bike Adventures

This name was chosen because it clearly communicates the site's focus on mountain biking experiences and adventures. It's straightforward, memorable, and instantly tells visitors what to expect from the website content.

Optional domain availability: mountainbike-adventures.com

Site Purpose

Mountain Bike Adventures serves as a comprehensive resource hub for mountain biking enthusiasts of all skill levels. The site provides:

  • Detailed trail guides and maps for popular mountain biking destinations
  • Equipment reviews and recommendations for bikes, gear, and accessories
  • Safety tips and skill-building tutorials for beginners to advanced riders
  • A community forum where bikers can share experiences and organize group rides
  • An events calendar featuring upcoming competitions, workshops, and group expeditions

Scenarios

Scenario 1: "I'm new to mountain biking and looking for beginner-friendly trails in my area. What criteria should I use to find appropriate trails for my skill level, and how can I prepare for my first few rides?"

Scenario 2: "I'm planning a mountain biking trip to Colorado this summer. What are the must-ride trails, and what specific gear should I bring for high-altitude riding that I might not need for my usual local routes?"

Scenario 3: "I'm considering upgrading my mountain bike. What are the key differences between trail bikes and enduro bikes, and which would be better for someone who enjoys both climbing and technical descents?"

Color Schema

The following colors have been selected to represent the outdoor adventure spirit of mountain biking while ensuring good readability and visual appeal:

#2e7d32

Primary Color (Forest Green)

Used for: Headers, navigation elements, and primary buttons

#ffab00

Secondary Color (Amber)

Used for: Accents, call-to-action elements, and highlights

Additional supporting colors include:

  • Light background: #f5f5f5 - Used for page backgrounds and light sections
  • Dark text: #333333 - Used for main body text to ensure readability

Typography

Montserrat

This font will be used for all headings (h1, h2, h3, etc.). Its bold, modern appearance creates strong visual hierarchy and complements the adventurous nature of mountain biking content.

Open Sans

This font will be used for body text, paragraphs, and general content. It offers excellent readability across different devices and screen sizes while maintaining a clean, professional appearance.
